Pages or Screens
----------------
* api_listing_screen: Displays data fetched from APIs, such as coffee and post listings.
* authentication_screen: Handles user login, registration, and authentication flows.
* home_screen: Main dashboard or landing area after successful authentication.
* landing_screen: Initial welcome or introduction page for the app.
* navigation_screen: Manages navigation and routing between different screens.
* splash_screen: Shows a splash/loading screen when the app starts.
* search_screen:searching 
* paginated_home_screen: Displays a paginated list of items.



widgets or components 
---------------------

* common_textfield: A reusable text field widget with customizable properties like hint text, validation, and styling.
*  custom_dropdown_field: A reusable dropdown field widget.
*
* not_found_widget: Displays a user-friendly message or graphic when no data or results are found.
*
* custom_tabbar_with_view.dart: Custom Tabbar and Tabview
* rating_star: A widget to display and handle star-based rating functionality.
* AnimatedGridBuilder: A widget to build animated grids with smooth transitions and dynamic content updates.
* app_links package added and andriodmanifest updated with app_links
* carousel_view_widget: A widget to display a carousel of images or items with swipe functionality.
